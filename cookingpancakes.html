Cooking Pancakes With GhostJelly

classical "english" pancakes or crepes depending on where your from



Recipe



Flour - 100g
Eggs - 2
Milk - 300g
Oil - 1tbsp
Salt - 1 pinch

Directions

1. Pour Ingredients Into A Bowl

2. Mix Until Smooth

*if its not smooth after mixing, leave it for 10 minutes

3. Cook For 1min 30sec on Each Side

4. Cook For The Entire World And End World Hunger
